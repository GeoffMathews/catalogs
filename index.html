Various datasets assembled from catalogs, to use with making figures and models for gen-ed Astronomy

Galactic

  1. Visible stars in XYZ coordinates (parsecs) centered on Earth. Vmag <= 4 from Hipparcos, hosted on Vizier
  2. Visible stars in RA/Dec as seen from Earth. Vmag <= 4 from Hipparcos, hosted on Vizier
  3. Globular clusters, positions in XYZ (kiloparsecs) centered on galactic center. From HEASARC, hosted by NASA

Solar systen

  1. Long-period comet aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center
  2. Asteroid aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center
  3. KBO aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center
