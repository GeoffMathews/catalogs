Various datasets assembled from catalogs, to use with making figures and models for gen-ed Astronomy

Galactic

  1. Visible stars in XYZ coordinates (parsecs) centered on Earth. Vmag <= 4 from Hipparcos, hosted on Vizier (top). Top down view , Side view
  2. Visible stars in RA/Dec as seen from Earth. Vmag <= 4 from Hipparcos, hosted on Vizier
  3. Globular clusters, positions in XYZ (kiloparsecs) centered on galactic center. From HEASARC, hosted by NASA. Top down view, centered on Earth

Solar systen

  1. Long-period comet aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center. Top down view
  2. Asteroid aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center. Full-sky view from Earth
  3. KBO aphelia, first 100 discovered. From JPL Minor Body Center. Top down view
